## About

I've job-hunted multiple times over my career, and every time the process has gotten
harder. Right now I have more direct experience with it than I'd like — I've been out
of work for over two years.

The breaking point was time, not effort. I was spending hours every day applying to
listings that were still posted but no longer active — jobs that had already been
filled weeks earlier. Eventually I started manually checking every LinkedIn listing
against the hiring company's own career page before I'd invest in the real work:
researching the company, tailoring a resume and cover letter, submitting, tracking.
That verification step, done one listing at a time by hand, is exactly what AI Job
Scout automates and scales.

I'm building this because that manual process worked — it just cost me hours I
should have spent networking and building skills instead. This tool exists so that
time goes back to where it belongs.

## How It Works

**1. Tell it what you're looking for**
Paste your resume as text, then fill in your target roles, location preference
(ranked), and company-fit signals — no file upload needed for v1.

**2. Get a researched shortlist of 30 companies**
AI Job Scout generates candidates from its own knowledge, then runs a live web
search pass on each one to spot-check headcount growth, company size, and
leadership turnover — citing sources along the way. This is AI-researched and
spot-checked, not a guaranteed-accurate verified database — treat it as a strong
starting point, not gospel.

**3. Curate your list**
Add companies it missed, cut ones that aren't a fit. You end up with a shortlist
you actually trust, built in minutes instead of the hours it used to take to
hand-vet listings one by one.
