# Outbound Drafts — AI Job Scout

Three short outreach messages for this week. All are honest about AI Job Scout being a v1, personal project: it currently takes a pasted resume + target-role preferences and returns an AI-researched, spot-checked 30-company shortlist (headcount growth, size, leadership stability). The live site's own copy still frames the self-serve version as "in progress" (see the mailto CTA on the homepage), so these drafts have Ted running the shortlist personally for early testers rather than promising a public self-serve tool that doesn't exist yet. They do **not** claim career-page monitoring, email alerts, resume tailoring, or Kanban tracking are live — those are v2, still in progress, per `PRD.md`.

## 1. To a potential beta user

**Subject:** Want a shortlist of companies actually worth applying to?

Hi [Name],

I built a small tool called AI Job Scout after burning hours every week researching companies instead of applying. It's v1, personal, still early: you send me your resume text plus target role, location, and company-size preferences, and the tool generates 30 candidate companies, then spot-checks each one against headcount growth, size, and leadership stability using live web search, citing sources. Not a job-board scrape — an actual researched shortlist.

No monitoring, alerts, or resume tailoring yet — that's next, still building. Just the shortlist, today.

**CTA:** Want to try it? Reply with your resume text and target role/location, and I'll send back your 30-company list.
