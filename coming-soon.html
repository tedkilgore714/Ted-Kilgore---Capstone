v1 stops at a researched, curated shortlist of companies. Here's what's planned next, once that foundation is solid.

Daily career-page monitoring + email alerts

Once your shortlist is set, AI Job Scout checks each target company's own career page every day and emails you the moment a new, active opening appears — with a direct link, key company context, and the reasoning behind the match, so you can act the same day it's posted.

Resume upload with parsing

Skip the copy-paste: upload your resume as a PDF or DOCX and have it parsed automatically, instead of pasting it in as plain text like v1 requires.

Tailored resumes and cover letters

For roles you decide to pursue, generate a resume and cover letter draft tailored to that specific posting, delivered as an editable .DOCX you can review and refine before you submit.

Application tracking (Kanban)

Track every opportunity's status — identified, applied, interviewing, closed — on a simple Kanban board, so nothing you're pursuing falls through the cracks.
