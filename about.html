About

I've job-hunted multiple times over my career, and every time the process has gotten harder. Right now I have more direct experience with it than I'd like — I've been out of work for over two years.

The breaking point was time, not effort. I was spending hours every day applying to listings that were still posted but no longer active — jobs that had already been filled weeks earlier. Eventually I started manually checking every LinkedIn listing against the hiring company's own career page before I'd invest in the real work: researching the company, tailoring a resume and cover letter, submitting, tracking. That verification step, done one listing at a time by hand, is exactly what AI Job Scout automates and scales.

I'm building this because that manual process worked — it just cost me hours I should have spent networking and building skills instead. This tool exists so that time goes back to where it belongs.
