How It Works

1. Tell it what you're looking for

Paste your resume as text, then fill in your target roles, location preference (ranked), and company-fit signals — no file upload needed for v1.

2. Get a researched shortlist of 30 companies

AI Job Scout generates candidates from its own knowledge, then runs a live web search pass on each one to spot-check headcount growth, company size, and leadership turnover — citing sources along the way. This is AI-researched and spot-checked, not a guaranteed-accurate verified database — treat it as a strong starting point, not gospel.

3. Curate your list

Add companies it missed, cut ones that aren't a fit. You end up with a shortlist you actually trust, built in minutes instead of the hours it used to take to hand-vet listings one by one.
